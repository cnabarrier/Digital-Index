⚖️ Primary Legislation

Acts of Parliament — top-level laws establishing CASA and authorising all subordinate regulations.

Civil Aviation Act 1988 (CAA)
Act
The principal Act. Establishes CASA, provides for AOCs. Section 3A: maintaining, enhancing and promoting aviation safety.
Federal Register →
Airspace Act 2007
Act
Ensures Australian airspace is administered safely. Requires an Airspace Policy Statement reviewed every 3 years.
Federal Register →
Aviation Transport Security Act 2004
Act
Aviation security framework — screening, security programs, security-controlled areas.
Federal Register →
Civil Aviation Safety Regulations 1998 (CASR)
Regulation
The principal subordinate regulations made under the Civil Aviation Act 1988. Covers certification, operations, licensing and more.
Federal Register →
